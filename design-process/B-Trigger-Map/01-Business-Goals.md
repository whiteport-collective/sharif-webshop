# Business Goals & Objectives

> Strategic goals and measurable objectives for Sharif.no

**Document:** Trigger Map - Business Goals
**Created:** 2026-03-26
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Vision

**Sharif.no makes buying tires the easiest thing a Norwegian car owner does all year — at prices the big chains can't match. One continuous guided experience, AI-powered operations, local mounting. Dominate every market we enter.**

---

## Business Objectives

### PRIMARY GOAL: Local Market Domination (THE ENGINE)

- **Statement:** Become the #1 online tire destination in every area where Sharif has a shop or partner workshop — crushing the competition on accessibility, usability, likability, and price
- **Metric:** Online order volume vs. local competitors in covered areas
- **Target:** Majority of Sharif's sales shift from phone to online within 3 months of launch
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch per covered area
- **Impact:** This drives ALL other objectives. Every area dominated proves the model for the next expansion.

---

### GROWTH GOALS (Driven by Local Domination)

**Objective 2: Scale Through Workshop Partnerships**
- **Statement:** Expand reach nationally by adding partner workshops — each new partner unlocks a new geo-targeted market
- **Metric:** Number of active partner workshops
- **Target:** 5-10 partner workshops within 12 months of V1 launch
- **Timeline:** 12 months

**Objective 3: Operational Leverage Through AI**
- **Statement:** Run the entire online business with minimal human overhead using AI agents for inventory, pricing, and content
- **Metric:** Moohsen's time spent on store operations per week
- **Target:** Less than 30 minutes per week
- **Timeline:** From launch

**Objective 4: Margin Control Through Direct Import**
- **Statement:** Maintain the price advantage by importing directly from Chinese factories, cutting out intermediaries
- **Metric:** Average margin per tire vs. competitors sourcing through distributors
- **Target:** Maintain lowest-price position in every covered area
- **Timeline:** Ongoing

---

### FUTURE GOALS (V2 Expansion)

**Objective 5: Nationwide Reach Through Shipping**
- **Statement:** Offer on-demand shipping from local suppliers for customers outside workshop coverage areas
- **Metric:** Orders from outside covered workshop areas
- **Target:** TBD — dependent on shipping cost economics
- **Timeline:** V2

**Objective 6: Premium Segment (Magnus)**
- **Statement:** Serve premium brand customers through on-demand supplier ordering
- **Metric:** Premium brand order volume
- **Target:** TBD
- **Timeline:** V2

## The Flywheel: How Goals Connect

**THE ENGINE (Priority #1):**
- Dominate locally in every covered area — best price, best experience, mounting included
- Harriet orders online instead of calling or going to a random local shop
- Every satisfied customer becomes word-of-mouth in a local community
- Phone orders shift to online — Moohsen scales without hiring

**GROWTH (Priority #2):**
- Proven local success attracts workshop partners
- Each partner adds a new area to dominate
- Geo-targeted ads in each new area drive traffic
- AI agents handle the increased volume without additional staff

**EXPANSION (Priority #3):**
- V2 adds shipping for customers outside workshop areas
- Premium brands attract Magnus (higher margin per tire)
- National reach without national infrastructure
