## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet books a time for mounting. This is the reward step — she's already paid, now she picks a time. The tone is celebratory and effortless.

**Entry Context:** Payment confirmed by Klarna/Qliro. She's redirected back to Sharif.no with order reference. The page greets her with confirmation.

**Exit Action:** Selects a time slot and confirms → booking confirmation shown. Scenario complete. ✓

## On-Page Interactions

- **Confirmation celebration** — "Takk for bestillingen!" with order summary
- **Calendar selector** — available time slots from Google Calendar (POC). Visual calendar with tappable slots.
- **Slot selection** — tap a time → it highlights
- **Confirm booking** — "Bekreft tid" button
- **Skip option** — "Velg tid senere" (receive email reminder) — in case she's in a hurry
- **Confirmation state** — after booking: checkmark, date/time, shop address, "Vi ses!"

## Design Notes

- This is the REWARD. The tone shifts from functional to celebratory.
- The "Takk!" moment should feel like a small win — subtle confetti animation? A satisfying checkmark? Keep it tasteful — not over-the-top, but memorable.
- The calendar should be dead simple — show available slots for the selected shop. No complex date picker.
- Saturday slots should be prominent — that's when Harriet is free.
- The "Velg tid senere" escape hatch is important — she might be rushing back to a client. Email reminder follows.
- After booking, the confirmation should include everything she needs: date, time, address. Screenshot-friendly.
- "Vi ses!" closes the experience with warmth. The hard-working father, quick to a smile.
- This page returns to the Sharif brand surface — not the payment provider. The retro racing aesthetic is back.
