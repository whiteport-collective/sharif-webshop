## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet pays for her tires. This is the clean hand-off to the external payment provider.

**Entry Context:** She tapped "Betal nå — 1 996 kr" on the quantity/shop view. Klarna/Qliro checkout loads.

**Exit Action:** Completes payment → redirected back to Sharif.no booking view (01.6)

**On-Page Interactions:**
- This view is controlled by Klarna/Qliro — not custom-designed
- Payment provider handles: customer identification, payment method, private/company toggle, address collection
- Sharif passes: order details, total amount, return URL
