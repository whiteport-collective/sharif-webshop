## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet browses matching tires and picks one.

**Entry Context:** She entered her dimension and tapped "Finn dekk". The parallax surface transitions down to reveal product cards.

**Exit Action:** Taps a product card → product detail overlay opens (01.3)

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Horizontal swipe through product cards — sorted by price, cheapest first
- Filter toggle — "Filter" button reveals collapsed overlay for brand and season filtering
- Stock badge on each card — green/amber/grey
- EU label mini-sliders on each card — Drivstoff, Veigrep, Støy as visual bars
