## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet enters her tire dimensions — the entry point to the buying flow.

**User Situation:** Between clients at the salon, 5 minutes to spare, phone in hand. She's never looked at a tire sidewall before.

**Mental State:**
- **Hope:** Get this sorted fast, find cheap tires
- **Worry:** Will I understand what they're asking me?

**Discovery Method:** Geo-targeted Instagram ad ("Sommerdekk fra 499kr — montering inkludert") → taps through → lands here

**Entry Context:** First impression of Sharif.no. Top of the continuous parallax surface.

**Exit Action:** Enters dimension and taps "Finn dekk" → products appear below via parallax transition

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Tire guide overlay — taps "Hvor finner jeg dette?" to see visual guide with celebrity UGC content showing tire sidewall markings
- Hybrid input — type/paste "205/55R16" directly, or use three dropdown selectors (width/profile/rim) that sync with the text field
- Smart parsing — pasting "205/55 R16" or "205 55 16" fills the dropdowns automatically
- Language selector — NO/EN toggle in header
- Main menu — hamburger, access to About, Contact, Tire guide
- Shopping cart — cart icon with item count badge, persists across all views
