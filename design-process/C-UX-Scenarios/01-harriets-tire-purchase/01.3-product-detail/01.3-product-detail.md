# 01.3 — Product Detail Overlay

## Page Metadata

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Scenario** | 01: Harriet's Tire Purchase |
| **Page Number** | 01.3 |
| **Platform** | Mobile (iPhone, portrait) |

## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet reviews the tire she tapped — sees the AI-generated story, EU label sliders explained in plain language, price, and stock status. This is where she confirms "this is the one."

**Entry Context:** Tapped a product card from the card stack (01.2). The overlay slides up from the bottom, covering most of the screen but leaving a hint of the cards behind.

**Exit Action:** Taps "Jeg tar disse" → overlay transitions into the quantity/shop view (01.4)

## On-Page Interactions

- **Close overlay** — swipe down or tap X to return to product cards
- **Scroll within overlay** — the overlay is scrollable for longer content
- **EU label expand** — tapping a slider expands to show plain-language explanation
- **CTA button** — "Jeg tar disse" (red, sticky at bottom of overlay)

## Content Elements

| Element | Type | Content |
|---------|------|---------|
| Close button | Interactive | X or swipe-down to dismiss |
| Tire image | Visual | Larger version of card image (NanoBanana generated) |

## AI Product Story Example

> Et solid hverdagsdekk som ikke tapper lommeboken eller tanken. Bra grep i regnet, normalt støynivå, og holder lenge nok til at du glemmer at du har det. Perfekt for kjøring til jobb og butikk.

(A solid everyday tire that won't drain your wallet or your fuel tank. Good grip in the rain, normal noise level, and lasts long enough that you forget you have it. Perfect for driving to work and the shop.)

## EU Label Slider Detail

When tapped, each slider expands:

**Fuel (Drivstoff):** D-rating
> "Gjennomsnittlig drivstofforbruk — du merker ingen forskjell på bensinkostnaden."
> (Average fuel consumption — you won't notice a difference on your fuel bill.)

**Grip (Veigrep):** C-rating
> "Godt grep i regnet — bremser trygt i våte forhold."
> (Good grip in rain — brakes safely in wet conditions.)

**Noise (Støy):** 70 dB
> "Normalt dekk-støynivå. Du hører musikken din fint."
> (Normal tire noise. You can hear your music fine.)

## Design Notes

- Overlay slides up from bottom — bottom sheet pattern, familiar from mobile apps
- Background (product cards) blurs and darkens — overlay gets focus
- The AI story is the hero — not the specs. Harriet reads the story. Ole scrolls down to the sliders.
- CTA "Jeg tar disse" stays sticky at the bottom — always accessible
- The tone: warm, unfussy, like a friend explaining. Not a data sheet.
- Price remains prominent throughout — it's her main decision factor
