# 01.4 — Quantity & Shop

## Page Metadata

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Scenario** | 01: Harriet's Tire Purchase |
| **Page Number** | 01.4 |
| **Platform** | Mobile (iPhone, portrait) |

## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet picks how many tires and where to get them mounted. Two quick decisions, then she pays. This is the last step before the payment hand-off.

**Entry Context:** She tapped "Jeg tar disse" on the product detail overlay. The overlay transitions/morphs into this view — the product she selected is now a compact summary at the top.

**Exit Action:** Taps "Betal nå" → page loads Klarna/Qliro checkout (01.5)

## On-Page Interactions

- **Quantity toggle** — 2 or 4 tires. Large tap targets. Total price updates live.
- **Shop selector** — map pins or list. Fjellhamar and Drammen. Tap to select.
- **Order summary** — compact: product name, quantity, unit price, total
- **CTA button** — "Betal nå" with total price (red, full width, sticky bottom)

## Content Elements

| Element | Type | Content |
|---------|------|---------|
| Selected tire summary | Compact | Small tire image + "Powertrac Adamas H/P" + "499 kr/stk" |
| Quantity headline | Text | "Hvor mange?" |
| Quantity toggle | Interactive | [2] [4] — large buttons, 4 is default/pre-selected |
| Shop headline | Text | "Hvor vil du montere?" |
| Shop cards | Interactive | Two cards: Fjellhamar (address, distance), Drammen (address, distance) |
| Availability hint | Text | "Mange ledige tider denne uken" (builds confidence without committing to a time) |
| Order summary | Calculated | 4 x 499 kr = 1 996 kr |
| CTA button | Interactive | "Betal nå — 1 996 kr" (red, full width, sticky bottom) |

## Design Notes

- This view should feel like a natural continuation — the parallax surface has moved deeper
- The selected tire is now a small summary at top — decision is made, moving forward
- Quantity defaults to 4 (most common purchase). Changing to 2 is easy but not the default.
- Shop selection could use a mini map or simple cards — depends on whether location data (distance) is available. For POC, simple cards with address.
- The availability hint ("Mange ledige tider denne uken") reduces anxiety about booking without locking in a time. Booking happens after payment.
- Total price on the CTA button — no surprises
- This is the bottom of the guided flow. "Betal nå" is the clean cut to the payment system.
