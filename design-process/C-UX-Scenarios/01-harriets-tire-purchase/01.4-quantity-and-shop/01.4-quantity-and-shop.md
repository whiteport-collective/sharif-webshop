## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Harriet picks how many tires and where to get them mounted.

**Exit Action:** Taps "Betal nå — 1 996 kr" → page loads Klarna/Qliro checkout (01.5)

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Quantity toggle — 2 or 4 tires, total price updates live, 4 is default
- Shop selector — tap to select Fjellhamar or Drammen, shows address and availability hint
- Order summary — compact display of product, quantity, total, mounting location
