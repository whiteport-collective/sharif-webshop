## Working Relationship Context

### Stakes
**Level:** business

**What this means:**
This is a real client project for Sharif AS (Norway). Moohsen Sharif has been trying to get this built since 2019. The project represents a significant investment (3 containers of tires, ~6,500 units) and a shift from phone-based sales to online. The relationship is long-standing (7 years of contact) and trust-based.

**Stakeholders:**

**Political Sensitivities:**
Moohsen has difficulty making decisions (noted by Mårten). The approach is to build a modern system and guide him through it rather than wait for specifications. The strategy is to keep things simple and actionable.
