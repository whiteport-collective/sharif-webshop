## Positioning Statement

> **For** Norwegian car owners who need tires but don't want the hassle,
> **Sharif** is the **online tire store** that lets you
> **buy tires and book fitting in minutes — at prices the big chains can't match.**
> **Unlike** Dekkmann, Vianor, and faceless online shops,
> **Sharif** combines **60+ years of tire expertise with the simplest digital buying experience in Norway** — backed by a growing network of local fitting workshops.

### Breakdown

- **Target:** Norwegian car owners (primarily price-conscious, tire-ignorant)
- **Need:** Buy tires without confusion, at a good price, with fitting included
- **Category:** Online tire store with local fitting
- **Key benefit:** Easiest buying experience + best prices
- **Differentiator:** Nationally recognized brand since the 60s + guided mobile-first flow + growing workshop network

## Key Signals from Conversation

**Brand strength:** Sharif is top of mind for most Norwegians in populated areas. Established since the 1960s — 60+ years of reputation. This isn't a startup disrupting — it's a trusted brand going digital.

**Competitive landscape:**
- Big chains: Dekkmann, Vianor, Euromaster — corporate, impersonal
- Online players: Various — clunky sites, no fitting service
- Sharif's edge: Known brand + cheapest prices (direct China import) + simplest UX + fitting included

**Growth model:**
- V1: Prove locally (own shops in Fjellhamar/Drammen)
- V2: National collaboration with workshop chains — each partner unlocks a new geo-targeted ad region (15 min driving radius)
- Franchise-like scaling without the franchise — Moohsen supplies tires + platform, partners do fitting
